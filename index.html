Data Cleaning and Analysis Project: Company Employee Dataset
As part of my portfolio, I have developed a comprehensive data cleaning and analysis project using Jupyter, Python, MySQL, and Power BI. The project involved working with a company employee dataset to extract valuable insights and visualize the data for meaningful interpretation.
The dataset consisted of employee information, including their IDs, names, birthdates, genders, races, departments, job titles, locations, hire dates, termination dates (if applicable), and office locations. With this dataset, I applied various data cleaning techniques to ensure data integrity and accuracy.
Using Jupyter and Python, I performed essential data cleaning operations such as handling missing values, removing duplicates, and correcting data types.
